三大增强感知系统剖析  

Analysis of three enhanced perception systems

01.

生物方位
触觉感知系统.

Biological orientation
tactile perception system. 

人工输入基于openCV的不同人脸识别数据,由AI进行监督机器学习并将标记的训练数据归类,再输出为不同分类决策.左中右三个位置的人脸数据分别对应分类1、2、3,经过自行训练后运行并打开摄像头.佩戴者自身通过不同方位振动马达的低频共振,感知到目标人体的所在位置; 同时,随着AI运算跟踪参与者面部移动位置,对应不同方位的LED灯亮起,慢慢地参与者会意识到,只要自己在装置附近,这些LED小灯会标记到你所在的位置.

Human input is based on different face recognition data of openCV, and AI will supervise machine learning and classify the labeled training data, and then output for different classification decisions. The wearer himself perceives the location of the target human body through the low-frequency resonance of the vibration motor in different directions; 

02.

方位立体声
感知系统.

Azimuth stereo
Perception system.

参与者一旦靠近装置,超声波传感器收到肢体动作的距离反馈后将自动调节鲸鱼音频的音效。所有距离数据均由数字编码实时处理。










Once the participant approaches the device, the ultrasonic sensor will automatically adjust the sound effect of the whale audio after receiving the distance feedback of the body movement. All distance data are processed in real time by digital coding.

03.

数据化
视觉系统.

Digital vision system


在这个装置里,我们通过超声波发射器运用时间差测距法将所监测到的距离信息传入Arduino,经过运算学习,分别传入对应左前、正前、右前方位的LED屏中.









In this device, we use the ultrasonic transmitter to use the time difference ranging method to transfer the monitored distance information to the Arduino, and after calculation and learning, it is passed into the LED screens corresponding to the front left, front, and front right positions.
